Providence is a congregation of saints who are united by the transforming Gospel of Jesus Christ, meeting weekly to worship the Triune God in spirit and in truth (John 4:24) and with reverence and awe (Heb 12:28). We are a biblical, confessional, and presbyterian church, and we would love to have you join us for worship! In the meantime, explore our site to learn more about us, listen to recent sermons, and find out about our upcoming events.
